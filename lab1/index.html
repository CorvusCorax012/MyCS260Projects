The Purest Form of Photography

Photography gives us a chance to capture a single moment in history. Moments that show people who lived and existed. Each photo is a memory and is more valuable than gold.

That being said, we take this for granted. We each carry a high quality camera in our pockets and snap photos like fat kids eat twix.

That is not the way we do things here at Vintage Photography. We choose the path of most resistance. We use only the oldest equipment we can find and do everything ourselves to develop photos. Nostalgia over Quality and Convenience!
